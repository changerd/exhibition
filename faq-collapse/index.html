Frequently Asked Questions

What is your return policy?

Our return policy allows returns within 30 days of purchase with a valid receipt. Items must be in their original condition.

How do I track my order?

You can track your order by logging into your account and visiting the "Order History" section. You will also receive an email with tracking information once your order has shipped.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we offer international shipping to most countries. Shipping fees and delivery times vary by location.

Can I change or cancel my order?

You can change or cancel your order within 24 hours of placing it by contacting our customer service team.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit cards, PayPal, and Apple Pay.

How do I apply a discount code?

You can apply a discount code at checkout by entering it in the "Promo Code" field and clicking "Apply."

What is your privacy policy?

Our privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information. You can read it in full on our Privacy Policy page.

How do I contact customer service?

You can contact our customer service team via email at support@example.com or by calling (123) 456-7890.

Do you offer gift cards?

Yes, we offer gift cards in various denominations. They can be purchased online and sent via email or postal mail.

How can I update my account information?

You can update your account information by logging into your account and visiting the "Account Settings" section.
